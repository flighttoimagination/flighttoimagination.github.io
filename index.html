McKenzie Paddock

Pointillist | Printmaker

I’m a transplant from New England, making my artist debut in Minnesota. The creative souls and community I’ve found during my cherished years here in the Twin Cities have made this place feel like home. Through my art, I hope to give back to community by helping people feel seen or offering the little dopamine boosts we’re all desperately needing these days. I’m passionate about process over product, working primarily with printmaking and pointillism. Each piece can take me countless hours of catharsis, frustration, experimentation, delayed gratification, existential crisis, and flow. What you see in each creation is only a sliver of the story so feel free to ask about the rest or fill it in with your own lived experience. I’m here to bring flight to imagination.

I'd love to connect with you!

I'm passionate about collaborating with others to bring imagined possibilities into the physical realm. Reach out with commission dreams, thoughts, questions, or requests.
